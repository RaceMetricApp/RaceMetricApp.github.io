Privacy Policy

RaceMetric is a local-first karting, race-weekend, fitness, and driver-performance tracking app by JH43 Racing.

Summary

RaceMetric is designed to work without an account. The app stores your driver, session, race-weekend, logger, fitness, circuit, and settings data locally on your device.

RaceMetric does not currently use third-party advertising, third-party analytics, cross-app tracking, or a developer-operated cloud account system.

RaceMetric may download read-only public category track-record reference data from RaceMetric's public GitHub data feed. This keeps track profiles accurate without requiring an app update, and it does not upload your RaceMetric data.

Information Stored In The App

RaceMetric may store information you enter or choose inside the app, including:

  • Driver profile details, display preferences, dashboard logo, and driver image.
  • Kart sessions, lap times, logger import summaries, setup notes, kart class, tyres, pressures, gearing, engine details, conditions, and race results.
  • Race weekends, planned sessions, reminders, notes, and checklist items.
  • Circuit details, custom circuit locations, track notes, and circuit history.
  • Simulator sessions.
  • Workouts, weight entries, reaction-time history, step goal, and daily step display.
  • Friend or rival names and comparison lap times when you choose to record them.
  • Local notification read state and reminder settings.
  • Coach videos or media you choose from your photo library.
  • Full backup JSON files when you choose to export them.

This information is stored locally on your device unless you choose to share or export it.

Permissions

Location

RaceMetric may ask for foreground location permission to help save circuit/session location context or support nearby circuit features. RaceMetric does not use background location tracking.

Motion And Fitness

RaceMetric may ask for Motion & Fitness permission to show your daily step count on the dashboard. Steps are used only for your own in-app activity view and do not affect the driver rating.

Photo Library And Videos

RaceMetric may ask for photo-library access so you can choose a dashboard logo, driver image, or coach video. RaceMetric uses the media item you select and does not scan your full library.

Files

RaceMetric may open a document picker so you can import supported logger files, such as UniPro TSV exports. The app reads the file you select so it can preview laps and create or update a RaceMetric session.

Notifications

RaceMetric may ask for notification permission so it can schedule local reminders for race weekends and planned sessions. These reminders are for app functionality, not advertising.

Sharing And Export

RaceMetric includes share/export features for summaries and local backups. When you use these features, RaceMetric opens your device share sheet so you can choose where the information goes.

Only share or export information to people and services you trust.

Public Reference Data

RaceMetric may connect to a public GitHub-hosted JSON file to refresh category track-record reference data shown in the Tracks area. This request is for read-only app content. RaceMetric does not add your session, logger, location, fitness, photo, profile, or settings data to this request.

Data Collection And Tracking

RaceMetric does not currently send your RaceMetric data to a developer-operated server.

RaceMetric does not sell personal data.

RaceMetric does not use third-party advertising SDKs.

RaceMetric does not use third-party analytics SDKs.

RaceMetric does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Data Deletion

You can edit or delete many entries inside RaceMetric. Removing the app from your device may also remove locally stored RaceMetric data. If you have exported a backup or shared data outside the app, you are responsible for managing those copies.

Children And Young Drivers

RaceMetric may be used by young karting drivers. A parent or guardian should supervise use of the app, especially when sharing summaries, exporting backups, choosing media, or contacting support.

Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated as RaceMetric changes. If future versions add accounts, cloud sync, analytics, or other data collection, this policy and the App Store privacy answers must be updated before release.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, contact racemetricapp@gmail.com.
